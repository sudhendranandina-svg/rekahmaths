Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal age to start abacus training?

Children between 6-12 years old benefit most from abacus training as their brains are most receptive to developing new neural pathways during this period.

Do I need to buy an abacus?

No, we provide a high-quality abacus kit as part of the course materials. This ensures all students learn with the same standardized tool.

How long does it take to see results?

Most children show noticeable improvement in calculation speed and accuracy within 2-3 months of regular practice.

Is online learning as effective as in-person?

Yes! Our online classes are interactive and engaging, with small group sizes ensuring personalized attention for each student.

What if my child struggles with the concepts?

We provide individualized support and adjust the pace according to each child's learning speed. No child is left behind!

Are there any competitions or certifications?

Yes, we prepare students for various abacus competitions and provide certificates upon completion of each level.
