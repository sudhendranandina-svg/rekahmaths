16 Powerful Vedic Sutras

Learn the ancient formulas that make complex calculations simple

1

Ekadhikena Purvena

By one more than the previous one

Example: 19 × 19 = 361 (in seconds!)
2

Nikhilam Navatashcaramam

All from 9 and the last from 10

Example: 97 × 96 = 9312 (mental calculation)
3

Urdhva-Tiryagbyham

Vertically and crosswise

Example: 23 × 47 = 1081 (one-line method)
4

Paravartya Yojayet

Transpose and adjust

Example: Division shortcuts for any number
5

Shunyam Saamyasamuccaye

When the sum is the same, that sum is zero

Example: Solve equations instantly
6

Anurupye Shunyamanyat

If one is in ratio, the other is zero

Example: Quick solutions for proportions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vedic Maths suitable for my child's school curriculum?

Absolutely! Vedic Maths complements all curricula (NZ, Cambridge, Oxford) by providing faster calculation methods that work with any mathematical concept.

How quickly will my child see improvement?

Most students notice significant speed improvements within 4-6 weeks of regular practice. Full mastery typically takes 6-9 months.

Will this help with competitive exams?

Yes! Vedic Maths is particularly beneficial for competitive exams where speed and accuracy are crucial, such as NCEA, SAT, and math olympiads.

Do students need any prior math knowledge?

Students should be comfortable with basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication tables up to 10). We build from there!

Can adults learn Vedic Maths too?

Definitely! We have adult students who use these techniques for professional development, competitive exams, and personal enrichment.

Are the techniques difficult to remember?

Not at all! Vedic techniques are based on logical patterns that become intuitive with practice. Most students find them easier than conventional methods.
